Simulated + Real Demo

Please complete the below steps to run the demo.

Switch on Xarm5

Turn on the switch on the controller box.

Release Emergency Stop Button

Pull the emergency stop button and wait for the sound on the robot.

Place red cube

Ensure the red cube is at the target marked on the table.

Remove any obtsacles

No obstacles should be near the workspace of the robot.

Safe Distance

Please maintain a safe distance from the robot while the demo is running.
